The Captain of Israel

  1. Michael is the Captain of Israel
  2. The AoY is the Captain of Israel

The Prince of Princes

The title, “Prince of Princes” is the same as “Lord of lords”. The Prince of princes is

  1. Jesus—the “Lord of lords” [Rev 17:14]
  2. Michael, the Prince of Israel—the “Prince of Princes” [Dan 8:25 cf., Dan 8:11]

The One Who Gave Israel Rest

God gave/promised to give Israel rest through

  1. The Angel of Yahweh [Exo 23:20-23; 33:14-17; Judges 2:1-4; Isa 62:9]
  2. The Captain of the Host who is the Archangel Michael [Jos 5:16-15 cf., Exo 23:20-23; 33:14-17; Judges 2:1-4]
    (Michael is the One who stands for Israel [Dan 12:1])

The Law and the Temple

The Law was given through, and the Temple was under the administration of

  1. The Angel of Yahweh [Act 7:38; Mal 3:1]
  2. The Captain of the Host who is the Archangel Michael [Dan 8:11]]

Direct Apostolic Statements

The One who accompanied Israel in the wilderness is was 

  1. The Angel of Yahweh
  2. Christ [1Cor 10:3]

The Glory of Yahweh

The divine being referred to as “the Glory of Yahweh” was

  1. The Angel of Yahweh
  2. The Lord Jesus Christ

The Gathering of Israel

The gathering of Israel is a personal concern to

  1. The Angel of Yahweh (Considering that he is their Captain and Lord and considering his relationship with Jacob, this is natural and expected)
  2. The Lord Jesus [Mat 23]

The Voice that Will Raise the Dead

The One whose voice will raise the dead is

  1. The Lord Jesus Christ [2Th ]
  2. The Voice of the Archangel Michael will raise the dead

The One the Antichrist Opposes

  1. The “Prince of Princes“ whom the Antichrist (i.e., the little horn of Daniel) will oppose is Michael for he is the Prince of the Host of Israel [Dan 8:25 cf., Dan 8:11]
  2. The “Lord of Lords” whom the Antichrist (i.e., the Beast of Revelation) will oppose is the Lord Jesus Christ [Rev 17:13-14; 19:19]
