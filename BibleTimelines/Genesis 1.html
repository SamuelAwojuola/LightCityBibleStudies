Where Did The Light Appear?

The light appeared on the surface of the deep
  1. The separation of light and darkness was time--each had its time. This means that they affected the same region.
  2. also, the fact that there was evening, which is darkness, and morning, which is light, also means that they affected the same region or else they could not be measured as evening and morning.
  3. Therefore, since the darkness was on the surface of the deep, that is the water below, as quite plainly stated, it means that the light shined on the surface of the deep as well.
Also, Paul speaks of the light shinning out of darkness,
For God, who said, “Let light shine out of darkness,” made his light shine in our hearts to give us the light of the knowledge of the glory of God in the face of Christ.[2Cor 4:6].
