There are two general periods with respect to God's anger against his people,

  1. The Period When God is Angry With His People [Isa 10:24,]
    1. During this period, God punishes His people over and over again [Isa 10:4]
    2. During this period, he will use Assyria to punish them [Isa 10:5]
  2. The Period when God's anger against His people has subsided [Isa 10:25]
    1. During this period, God will punish Assyria [Isa 10:25]
