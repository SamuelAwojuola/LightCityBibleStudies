Siege of Samaria

  • Hezekiah's 4th year was the 7th year of King Hoshea of Israel
  • Hoshea began to reign in the 12th year of Ahaz

  • Therefore, Hezekiah must have begun to reign before the end of the reign of Ahaz his father.
