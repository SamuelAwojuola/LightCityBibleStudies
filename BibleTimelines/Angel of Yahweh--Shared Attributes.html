Jesus Christ

  1. The Name of Yahweh
    1. Jesus has been given the Name that is above all other names [Joh 17:11; Php 2:9-10; Eph 1:21].
  2. The Presence/Face of Yahweh
    1. To see Jesus is to have seen the Father [Joh 14:7,9]
  3. The Glory of Yahweh
    1. Jesus is the Glory of Yahweh [Heb 1:3; 1Co 2; 2Co 4:4,6; Mat 17:2; Col 1:15,19]
  4. Superiority to All Others
    1. Jesus is superior to all other angels [Heb 1:4]
  5. Leader of Israel
    1. Jesus is the One appointed over Israel to lead them [Act 3:20]
    2. And he will lead them
  6. The Day of Yahweh
    1. Jesus will be the leader of Yahweh's army, Yahweh's angels in the day of Yahweh [Rev 19:11,14]

Jesus Christ & the Angel of Yahweh

All of the above facts of Scriptures show Jesus Christ to be the angel of Yahweh

  1. Since the above facts are attributes that can only be possessed by one person and
  2. Since both Jesus Christ and the Angel of Yahweh possess these attributes
  3. Then Jesus Christ and the Angel of Yahweh are the same persons

Therefore, Jesus Christ is the Archangel Michael since he is the Angel of Yahweh.

Michael & the Angel of Yahweh

  1. The Name of Yahweh
    1. Michael was called by the Name Yahweh when he appeared to Joshua [Jos 5:13-15; 6:2-5]
  2. Superiority to All Others
    1. Michael is greater than all other princes/captains. He was described as
      2. The Prince/Captain of Princes/Captains [Dan 8:11,25]
      3. The First of the Princes/Captains [Dan 10:13]
      4. The Great Prince/Captain [Dan 12:1]
  3. Leader of Israel
    1. Michael is the leader of Israel, "The Captain/Commander/Prince of the Host of Yahweh" [Jos 5:14; Dan 12:1]
  4. The Day of Yahweh
    1. Michael will give a shout on the day of Yahweh [1Thes 4:16]
  5. Resurrection
    1. The rising of Michael in the time of the end will lead to the resurrection of tn of the dead [Dan 12:1-3]
    2. (From the argument of Satan with Michael over the body of Moses) Michael raised Moses from the dead [Jud 1:9]

The Angel of Yahweh

  1. The Name of Yahweh
    1. He is called, over and over, by the Name of Yahweh
    2. In fact, Yahweh says that His Name is in him [Exo 23:21]
  2. The Presence/Face of Yahweh
    1. He is called the Face/Presence of God
      1. We can plainly see this from knowing that the it was the Angel of Yahweh with whom Jacob wrestled.
      2. Jacob called the place where he wrestled with the man "Peniel" meaning face of God and also said that he had seen God face to face [Gen 32:30]
      3. Hosea tells us that it was with an angel that Jacob wrestled [Hos 12:1-5]
  3. The Glory of Yahweh
    1. Jesus is the Glory of Yahweh [Heb 1:3; 1Co 2:8; 2Co 4:4,6; Mat 17:2; Col 1:15,19]
  4. Superiority to All Others
    1. The Angel of Yahweh is superior to all other angels
      1. In Jacob's vision of the stairway reaching to heaven, the Angel of Yahweh was at the head of the stairway with other angels ascending and descending upon the stairway [Gen 28:12-13]
      2. The testimony of the magicians of Egypt that Yahweh, who was in person the Angel of Yahweh with Moses, was God [Exo 8:19] pointing to his superiority to all other gods.
      3. Moses' insistence that the Angel of Yahweh (whom Yahweh described as His Presence)
  5. Leader of Israel
    1. The Angel of Yahweh was to guard, deliver and bring Israel into the promised land [Exo 23:20-23]
    2. He was the One through whom God brought the Israelites out of Egypt and
    3. By whose hand Yahweh led the people through the wilderness [Exo 13:21-22; 14:19]
    4. Even in the land he was to be with them and he was with them [Jdg 2:1-5]
  6. The Day of Yahweh
    1. The Angle of Yahweh will defend and lead Israel in the Day of Yahweh [Zec 12:8]
  7. Resurrection from the Dad
    1. Micah spoke of the Angel of Yahweh as being the first to rise from the dead and by so making the resurrection of the People of Israel possible [Mic 5:13]

Principality (i.e., Captain/Prince Over a People) Facts

A principality is a leader, a head.

His leadership can be over as few as 10, or a 100, or a 1,000 [Exo 18:21] and so on. And his leadership can be over men or angels.

  1. ἄρχων (archōn) in Greek
    1. Persia and Greece have their Archōn [Dan 10:13,20 ABP+ Septuagint]
    2. Michael is the Archōn of Israel [Dan 12:1 ABP+ Septuagint]
    3. After interpreting the writing on the wall, Daniel was declared to be the Third Archōn in Babylon [Dan 5:29]
  2. שׂר (śar) in Hebrew
    1. Prince, captain [Gen 12:1]
    2. Persia and Greece have their Archōn [Dan 10:13,20]
    3. Michael is the Śar of Israel [Dan 12:1]

The sons of God over the nations are the Principalities [Deut 32:8-9; Gen 11:7; Psa 82:1,6]. They are the highest ranking angels for they set over whole nations.

Blessing Facts

  1. God forbids us seeking the blessing of other gods.
  2. Blessing are to be and to be sought in the Name of Yahweh [Num 6:22-27].
  3. Yahweh's blessings are conditioned upon obedience to Him [Exo 23:25; Deut 7:12-15ff; 11:26-27ff; 15:4-5]
  4. Those that God has blessed are not to be cursed [Num 22:12; 23:20]

Blessing In/By

  1. The Angel of Yahweh
    1. Jacob sought the blessing of the Angel of Yahweh [Gen 23:26]
    2. Jacob blessed Joseph in the Name of the Angel of Yahweh [Gen 48:15-16]
    3. Yahweh promised to bless His people if they would submit to the Angel of Yahweh [Exo 23:20-23,25]
    4. Moses blessed Joseph by the Angel of Yahweh [Deut 33:16]
