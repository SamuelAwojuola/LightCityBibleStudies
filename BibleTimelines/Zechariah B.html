The Angels of Zechariah

The important angels to figure out

  1. The Angel of Yahweh on the Red Horse to whom the other horses reported. He is important because the horses sent out by Yahweh reported to him and because of his personal intercession for Jersualem.
  2. The Angel of the Third Vision who came with a word to be given to Zechariah (or the surveyor angel). He is important because he speaks as the Angel of Yahweh declaring that he will live among the Israelites and they will know that God sent him.
  3. The Angel of Yahweh of the fourth vision

The following are the number of angels that interact with Zechariah in each vision: Vision...

  1. One angel
  2. One angel, the same angel as in vision one
  3. Three angels
    1. The surveyor angel
    2. The Angel of the first and second vision
    3. The New Angel
  4. One angel, the Angel of Yahweh
  5. One angel--which of the preceding angels is this?
  6. One angel--same as angel of vision 5
  7. One angel--same as angel of vision 5
  8. One angel--same as angel of vision 5
